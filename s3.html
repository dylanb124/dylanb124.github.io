DESIGN CHOICES

TAKING A CLOSER LOOK AT DESIGNING AND ADAPTING RESEARCH RESULTS.

The Details

Designing For Multiple Genres

Ultimately we minimized all of our findings into three categories, graphic design, web architecture, and web content. These three categories could then be made more broad and could contain more information as the platform size increased, or in some cases, decreased.

Rhetorical Choices

Rhetorical tone was altered throughout the project based upon the audience and the marketing aspects of the material. The report used very scientific tones, the executive summary utilized a more informative and persuasive tone, while the tone used for the social media posts was more dialectic and engaging.

Marketing Approach Within Social Media

We chose to post on Facebook pages of two companies, GoDaddy and Website Design & Development Group, which are both organizations that host and build websites for customers. We chose to use graphics paired with engaging verbiage in order to catch the attention of users as well as encourage them to learn more about the importance of web design research and development.

Outcomes & Discoveries

The research showcased that the most important and impactful aspects of a website's design is the way the content is organized and the personalization systems used.
